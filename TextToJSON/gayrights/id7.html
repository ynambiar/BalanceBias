26 Sep 2015 05:43 AM EST

Malcolm Turnbull the new prime minister has long been an advocate for same-sex marriage, but is being accused of backflipping on his previous stance in order to keep happy the conservatives in his party. Many people gathered in Sydney Wednesday night to protest the newly-designated prime minister's stance on marriage equality. Despite lousy weather, people young and old attended the rally to show their support for the legalization of same-sex marriage in Australia. Among the guest speakers was Maya Newell, director of controversial film Gayby Baby, she said "I think Australia really just needs to step up to the plate and get on with it."
